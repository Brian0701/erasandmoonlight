Our Story

Built on the belief that great clothing transcends seasons — Eras and Moonlight dresses men and women for every chapter of their lives.

Who we are

Eras and Moonlight is a Philippine-based clothing label founded in 2022. We curate for anyone who moves between worlds — effortlessly, unapologetically. Our collections span casual, formal, and everything in between — designed for both men and women.

How we work

Our pieces are carefully sourced from trusted suppliers across Asia — Japan, Korea, China, and Thailand — bringing you quality fabrics and contemporary cuts at honest prices. We keep our sourcing transparent so you know exactly where your clothes come from.

Timeless

Designed beyond trends — pieces built to be worn across years, not seasons.

Asian-Sourced

Carefully selected from Japan, Korea, China, and Thailand. Quality you can feel from the first wear.

Inclusive

Sizes XS–2XL for women, S–XXL for men. Fits designed for real bodies.

Honest

Clear pricing, transparent sourcing, and straightforward returns — always.
